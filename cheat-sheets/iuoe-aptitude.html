Math Formulas — Memorize These

Percent grade: (Rise ÷ Run) × 100
Volume (rectangular): L × W × H
Volume (trench): Length × Width × Depth
Soil swell: Loose volume = Bank volume × (1 + swell %)
Soil shrinkage: Compacted = Bank × (1 - shrinkage %)
Production rate: Volume ÷ Time
Truck loads: Total volume ÷ Bucket or truck capacity
Cycles per hour: 3600 ÷ cycle time in seconds
Overtime pay: OT hours × Base rate × 1.5

Mechanical Reasoning — Rules of Thumb

Gears

  • Larger gear = slower, more torque. Smaller = faster, less torque.
  • Driven RPM = Drive RPM × (Drive teeth / Driven teeth)
  • Each meshed gear reverses direction. 3 gears in a row: gear 1 and gear 3 spin same direction.

Pulleys (Block & Tackle)

  • Mechanical advantage = number of supporting ropes.
  • 4 supporting ropes = 4× advantage = lift 400 kg with 100 kg force.
  • Trade-off: pull 4× more rope to lift it.

Levers

  • 1st class: Fulcrum in middle (crowbar, scissors)
  • 2nd class: Load in middle (wheelbarrow, bottle opener)
  • 3rd class: Effort in middle (fishing rod, tweezers, your arm)

Hydraulics

  • Force = Pressure × Area.
  • Area scales with diameter squared. Double piston diameter = 4× force.
  • Same pressure both ends, larger piston wins on force, smaller wins on speed/distance.

Reading Comprehension — Trap Words

  • AND = both conditions required. Miss one = no.
  • OR = either is enough.
  • SHALL / MUST = mandatory. Not "should" or "may."
  • NOT in the question — read it twice. "Which is NOT…" reverses the answer.
  • EXCEPT = find the one that doesn't fit.
  • Numbers in regs — when the question gives a distance/time, read carefully. "Within 3 m" excludes 4 m.

Spatial Reasoning — Quick Tricks

  • Rotation 90° clockwise: Up → Right → Down → Left → Up
  • Rotation 180°: Flips both axes. Up-right becomes down-left.
  • Operator orientation: Operator faces the boom. Their right = right side of machine.
  • Slope ratio (run:rise): 2:1 means 2 horizontal per 1 vertical. Gentler than 1:1.
  • Compass: N up, E right, S down, W left. "Northeast" = top-right.

Day-of-Test Walk-In List

  • Photo ID
  • 2 sharpened pencils + eraser
  • Eat protein, hydrate, no caffeine bomb
  • Read every question twice. Watch for NOT and EXCEPT.
  • If stuck, mark it and come back. Don't burn 5 minutes on one question.
  • Round numbers expected. Ugly decimals = recheck.
