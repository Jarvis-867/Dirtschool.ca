Load Calculation

Truck weight limit: Most dump trucks: 15-20 tons payload

Example: 15-ton truck ÷ 1.5 tons/cy (gravel) = 10 cubic yards per load

Weight vs Volume

When estimating material movement, remember: heavier materials = fewer cubic yards per truck load.

  • Topsoil: ~1 ton/cy → 15+ cy per truck
  • Clay: ~1.1 ton/cy → 13-14 cy per truck
  • Rock: ~1.5 ton/cy → 10 cy per truck
