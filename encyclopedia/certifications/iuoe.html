IUOE Apprenticeship: Union Operating Engineers

What is IUOE?

The International Union of Operating Engineers (IUOE) is the largest equipment operator union in North America. Represents 400,000+ members across US, Canada, and internationally.

IUOE Apprenticeship

Duration: 24 months (on-job + classroom)

Requirements: Age 18+, pass background check, physical exam

Classroom: ~120 hours/year (winter schedule, evenings)

On-job: ~2,000 hours/year (paid work on union jobsites)

Apprentice Wage Scale

LevelWage %Hourly (Alberta)
First 6 months50%~$28/hr
6-12 months60%~$34/hr
12-18 months75%~$43/hr
18-24 months90%~$52/hr
Journeyman (after)100%~$58/hr + benefits

Benefits (Journeyman)

  • Health plan (100% coverage for you + family)
  • Pension (employer contributes 5%+)
  • Vacation (3+ weeks)
  • Seniority protection (can't be fired unfairly)
  • Job security (guaranteed hours)

Regional Locals

Alberta: Local 955 (oil/gas, heavy civil). BC: Local 115 (construction). Ontario: Local 793 (utilities).

How to Apply

  1. Find local nearest you
  2. Contact apprenticeship coordinator
  3. Complete application (high school diploma + interest)
  4. Interview with union
  5. Pass background, physical, drug test
  6. Start apprenticeship
