NCCER: Heavy Equipment Operations Certification

What is NCCER?

The National Center for Construction Education & Research (NCCER) offers standardized, industry-recognized certifications. Curriculum is uniform across North America.

NCCER Heavy Equipment Program

Cost: $2,000-3,500 (training + testing)

Time: 6-12 weeks (typical)

Levels: Level 1 (basics), Level 2 (intermediate), Level 3 (advanced)

NCCER Curriculum

  • Equipment operation (dozers, excavators, loaders, graders)
  • Safety (OSHA, pre-trip, hazard identification)
  • Math and estimating
  • Materials and measurements
