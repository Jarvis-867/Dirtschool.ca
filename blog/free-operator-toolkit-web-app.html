You're on a job site with no cell signal. You need to calculate cut and fill volumes, track fuel consumption, and log your hours before you forget. Your phone is dead or spotty. What do you do?

That's exactly why we built the Operator Toolkit — and we made it completely free.

What Is the Operator Toolkit?

It's an offline web app built specifically for heavy equipment operators. No signup, no login, no BS. Just tools that solve real problems on the job site.

5 Essential Tools in One App

  • Cut & Fill Calculator — Calculate volumes in metric and imperial. Know how many cubic meters or yards you're moving before you bid.
  • Trench Volume Calculator — Width, depth, length. Get the tonnage and truck loads in seconds.
  • Fuel Efficiency Tracker — Log fuel in liters per hour (L/h) or gallons per hour (GPH). Track efficiency across jobs.
  • Daily Logbook — Hours worked, production, delays, notes. Export as CSV to email your boss or load into a spreadsheet.
  • Equipment Checklist — 10-item daily inspection checklist. Digital record of what you checked before you fired up the engine.

Why Offline Matters

Job sites kill your signal. You're in a cut, surrounded by dirt and rock. No 4G. No WiFi. Most apps are useless out there.

Our toolkit works completely offline. Once you load it in your browser (phone or tablet), it runs locally on your device. No internet required.

You can use it in remote locations, on planes, in basements — anywhere. And since it syncs to your device's local storage, your data doesn't go anywhere.
